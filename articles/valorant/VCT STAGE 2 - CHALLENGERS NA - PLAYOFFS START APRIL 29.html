VCT STAGE 2 - CHALLENGERS NA - PLAYOFFS START APRIL 29

After two sets of open qualifiers and two accompanying main events, it’s time to determine the two teams that will represent North America at Masters: Reykjavik.

Here’s everything you need to know to watch the 2021 VCT Stage 2 - Challengers NA - Playoffs.

TOURNAMENT OVERVIEW

VCT Stage 2 - Challengers NA - Playoffs is the last North American tournament for Stage 2 of the inaugural VALORANT Champions Tour: a year-long global circuit featuring three tiers of competition (Challengers, Masters, and Champions) across North America, South America, Europe, and Asia.

The eight teams competing in VCT Stage 2 - Challengers NA - Playoffs qualified through two weeks of Challengers NA events. Now, the two tournament finalists will advance to Masters: Reykjavik, the first international LAN in VALORANT Esports history.

VCT Stage 2 - Challengers NA - Playoffs will be a double-elimination bracket featuring best-of-three matches and a best-of-five grand final. The upper bracket finalist will be given the choice to either pick the grand final’s first two maps or maps three and four. The winner will be crowned North American Champion.

COMPETING TEAMS

The following eight North American teams will compete at the VCT Stage 2 - Challengers NA - Playoffs:

100 Thieves
Andbox
Cloud9
NRG
Sentinels
Team Envy
Version1
XSET

BROADCAST SCHEDULE & CO-STREAMING

VALORANT NA and Nerd Street Gamers will provide official broadcast streams of every match in the VCT Stage 2 - Challengers NA - Playoffs. With matches being played simultaneously on Thursday and Friday, we’ve organized the streams to make sure you can find each one. All matches are open to be co-streamed by registering and following our co-streaming guidelines.

To make it easy to remember, each day begins at 1 PM PT.

VCT Stage 2 - Challengers NA - Playoffs ALT: twitch.tv/valorant_esports_na OR twitch.tv/nerdstgamers2
